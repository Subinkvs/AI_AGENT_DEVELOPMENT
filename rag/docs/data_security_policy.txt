Data Security and Confidentiality Policy
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: January 2026

Employees must protect company data and confidential information at all times.
Sharing sensitive company information with unauthorized individuals is strictly prohibited.
All company systems must be accessed using secure authentication methods.
Company equipment and systems are to be used strictly for work-related purposes.
Installing unauthorized software on company devices is not allowed.
Any misuse of company resources may lead to disciplinary action.
