Company Policies Document
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: January 2026

1. Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional behavior.
Harassment, discrimination, or unethical conduct of any kind will not be tolerated.
Employees must treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect at all times.

2. Working Hours and Attendance
The standard working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday through Friday.
Employees are expected to log in on time and inform their manager in advance if they are unable to attend work.
Flexible working hours may be approved by managers based on role requirements.

3. Remote Work Policy
Employees are eligible for remote work up to three days per week, subject to manager approval.
Remote employees must ensure a stable internet connection and maintain productivity during working hours.
The company reserves the right to revoke remote work privileges if performance is impacted.

4. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 18 paid leave days per year.
Unused leave cannot be carried forward unless explicitly approved by management.
Medical leave requires submission of a valid medical certificate.

5. Data Security and Confidentiality
Employees must protect company data and confidential information at all times.
Sharing sensitive company information with unauthorized individuals is strictly prohibited.
All company systems must be accessed using secure authentication methods.

6. Use of Company Resources
Company equipment and systems are to be used strictly for work-related purposes.
Installing unauthorized software on company devices is not allowed.
Any misuse of company resources may lead to disciplinary action.

7. Disciplinary Action
Violation of company policies may result in warnings, suspension, or termination depending on severity.
The company follows a fair and transparent disciplinary process.

End of Document
